Health & Wellness

  • Aina | Salon And Spa
  • Finesse | Fitness Centre

Relax, restore & rejuvenate with our wellness facilities at The Alcor. With two luxurious spas, beauty salons & one fitness centre, we want to offer our guests the opportunity to have access to all wellness facilities under one roof, without worrying about stepping out of the property.

AINA | SALON AND SPA

With a plethora of skin, hair & body treatments – Aina is a sanctuary for relaxation. With two elegantly designed outlets, separate for men and women, Aina offers you the luxury of stepping down from your rooms to get all your salon or spa needs taken care of.

Our beauty salon offers hair and skin treatments as well as bridal services for wedding clients such as makeup, draping, styling & more. With highly trained therapists and reputed amenities, our spa offers a variety of relaxing massage treatments.

FINESSE | FITNESS CENTRE

The Alcor Gymnasium is located on the second floor of the hotel and is open from Monday – Saturday, with timings: 7am – 1pm & 5pm – 9pm. Finesse is a serene and refreshing space housing all the latest fitness equipment and welcoming hotel guests as well as memberships.
