Let us take you on a gastronomical adventure, a chance to experience flavours & palates from all around the world. We are experimentalists at heart & we love bringing new creations and cuisines to our beloved clients. With the following dining and lounging options, we encourage you to savour the best of what Alcor has to offer.

Nook | 24 Hour Coffee Shop

With a wide cuisine selection including Continental, Mexican, Italian, Mediterranean, Spanish, French, German, South Indian & quick bites, Nook is all about getting what you want, when you fancy it. Breakfast, lunch, dinner, brunch, tea or maybe a midnight snack – guests are welcome to walk-in at any time of the day for buffet or À la carte options.

Zirca: 1st Floor
View Menu

Zirca | Indian Restaurant

Traditionalists at heart, Zirca is our way of introducing India to the world. With delicate spices, razor sharp blends and an exquisite menu, dining is an art here. At Zirca, we offer authentic & fusion creations of our Indian cuisine – with a startling variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Discover our culinary creations, tantalize your taste buds, and enjoy the timelessness of these flavours – at Zirca.

Nook: G Floor
View Menu

Oriental Flavours | Far Eastern Cuisine

Let’s take you on a journey into the far-eastern cuisine. An unforgettable culinary experience – with the best of Thai, Burmese, Japanese Chinese, Korean, Vietnamese, Tibetan & more flavours that we have to offer. Guests are welcome to enjoy the quaint ambience and witness live cooking at our Teppanyaki counter.

Oriental: 1st Floor
View Menu

Sweet Talk | Patisserie

Sweet Talk offers a variety of sweetmeats such as cakes, cookies, chocolates, croissants, breads, buns, and our very special, in-house Italian gelato and frozen yoghurt. The bakery welcomes special orders for any kind of occasion, being festive, celebratory or business and we also offer sugar free, gluten free and vegan options for our conscious patrons.

Sweet Talk: G Floor
View Menu

The Oakwood | Bar And Lounge

Furnished with warm tones, wooden flooring and comfortable couches, this spacious and beautifully curated bar and lounge delivers you the electric atmosphere of a gastro pub and sets the tone for an inviting ambience. The Oakwood also offers an impeccable selection of fine liquors to its discerning guests.

Oakwood: 1st Floor
View Menu

The Royal Oak | Private Bar And Lounge

Our private bar and lounge is a pleasantly elegant and gorgeously lavish up-scale bar with contemporary décor and tasteful artifacts. It is the perfect space for guests looking to unwind in a peaceful ambience with their favorite drinks and snacks.

Royal Oak: 1st Floor
