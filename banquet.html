For all your special milestones & celebrations, we have both indoor and outdoor venue options at Alcor, to help you curate unforgettable moments.

Looking to celebrate from the comfort of your home? Our outdoor catering services allows our MasterChef’s to curate handcrafted menus for you, with a team of banquet managers ensuring personalized service – all from the comfort of your home.

To discuss your requirements, please reach out to us at banquet@alcorhotels.com

Alcove I & Rooftop

This grand ballroom has a separate lobby entrance with lift access & an area for parking. It is well-lit with live food counters & also has the advantage of an adjoining rooftop to provide a good mix of an indoor and outdoor venue flow.

Size: Alcove I: 372 Sq. Mt | Rooftop: 325 Sq. Mt

Alcove II

Located in the basement, this hall has a pre-function area along with live food counters, a dance floor setup & a projector screen. This free-flowing venue is good for corporate events as well as social gatherings

NSize: 280 Sq. Mt
Basement Floor

Professional I & II

This compact venue doubles up as a business center for corporate events as well as a perfect venue for intimate social gatherings. It has been designed with a designated food & beverages section in order to provide a separate area for refreshments and some live food. Audiovisual equipment, projector screenand conferencing systems are available upon request.

Size: 306 Sq. Mt
2nd Floor

Conference Room

A ten to twelve seater board room with Wi-Fi connection & plug points, useful for companies that seek to rent our co-working space for meetings or interviews.

2nd Floor

Cloud 8 Rooftop

Located on the eight floor of our hotel, Cloud 8 is an innovative space with a breathtaking view of our steel city, adorned with beautifully crafted ‘gazebos’’ that offer comfortable seating arrangement.

Size: 230 Sq. Mt
8th Floor

Front Lawn

Our richly landscaped front lawn has become a hotspot for a variety of events and occasions. This green expanse is a favorite amongst our guests looking for a warm and comfortable gathering venue, especially during the winter season!

Size: 230 Sq. Mt

Back Lawn

This sprawling expanse can be transformed into a grand outdoor venue for all your special occasions. Exclusively offered to patrons looking to curate a luxurious experience, it is an ideal venue for large scale social as well as corporate events.

Size: 790 Sq. Mt
